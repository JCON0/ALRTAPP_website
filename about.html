About ALRTAPP

Built for people who
look out for each other

ALRTAPP is a community-first personal safety platform designed for real life: commuting, travelling, nights out, and everyday movement. We connect trusted contacts with local awareness so people can make faster, safer decisions when it matters most.

People-first
Designed around real-world safety behavior and trust networks
Real-time
Fast alerts and live visibility when situations change
Local
Community context to help you avoid risk nearby
Simple
Clear UX with low friction in high-stress moments
Mission
Help people feel safer and more connected by combining trusted-circle tools with live community intelligence.
Vision
A future where personal safety is proactive, accessible, and powered by collective care.
Status
ALRTAPP is currently in development. We’re refining features with early supporters before launch.
